## SRCE-HANDLING

Free-text routing note for SRCE relevance of an SRYSO repertoire row.

Blank means no SRCE action or relevance is currently indicated.

Nonblank means the row contains SRCE-related status, routing, or follow-up information worth reading.

Examples:

- `already there`
- `review for SRCE`
- `use SRYSO repository`
- `port metadata only`
- `possible trio arrangement; inspect parts`
- `possible chamber arrangement; inspect parts`
- `do not duplicate files; link/reference only`

This field does not determine SRCE suitability. It only records known SRCE-related handling.
